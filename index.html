About Sharon

Sharon Shaffer, has been teaching voice for 30 years. Her voice students have gone on to major colleges and conservatories, are singing on Broadway, in Nashville and in LA. Locally her students are soloists and performing with; The Cleveland Orchestra and Chorus, The Cleveland Opera, the Beck Center, CPT, Dobama, Cleveland Musical Theater Project, Pickwick and Frolic and many more…

Ms. Shaffer has had a prolific performance career with decades of professional performing experience. She has appeared in leading operatic and musical theater roles, with orchestras and theater companies, and in recitals throughout the United States, Europe and Scandinavia. Sharon is an alumna of The University of Michigan, Oberlin Conservatory of music, and Cleveland State University respectively and holds a BM in voice and an MMA in voice, vocal pedagogy with a concentration in Choral Conducting.

In addition to teaching Ms. Shaffer recently finished her eight-year tenure as the Conductor/Artistic director of HaZamir Cleveland, and an Associate Conductor of HaZamir, The International Jewish High School Choir, in NYC. She is pleased to have been able to conduct numerous times in Lincoln Center and made her conducting debut in Carnegie hall and the Metropolitan Opera with the group.

Presently Ms. Shaffer is choral conductor of The Park Synagogue Choir in Cleveland Heights, and maintains a busy private teaching studio in Shaker Heights Ohio.
